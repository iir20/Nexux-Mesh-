# Trademark Policy & Brand Guidelines

## Purpose
Protects the integrity, representation, and public image of the NEXUS MESH brand, its logos, symbols, and official names.

## Scope
Defines permissible and non-permissible uses of the name "NEXUS MESH" and its accompanying logos.

## Permitted Usage
- Describing your product's integration or compatibility with NEXUS MESH.
- Distributing unmodified builds of the open-source application with original branding.

## Restricted Usage
- Using "NEXUS MESH" in the name of a commercial fork or distinct product line.
- Creating websites, services, or documentation that falsely imply official sponsorship or endorsement by the core project.
