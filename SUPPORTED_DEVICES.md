# Supported Android Devices & Compatibility Matrix

## Hardware Targets
- **Google Pixel Series**: (Pixel 6, 7, 8 Pro) - Dynamic BLE caching operates with minimal latency.
- **Samsung Galaxy Series**: (S21, S22, S23, S24) - High Wi-Fi Direct connection stability.
- **Generic Android One**: Supported above API level 26.

## Non-Supported/Degraded Hardware
- Devices lacking BLE peripheral advertising capabilities.
- Non-Google play devices blocking custom permissions.
