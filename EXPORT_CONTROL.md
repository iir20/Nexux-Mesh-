# Export Control & Cryptographic Classification Compliance

## Purpose
Ensures legal compliance with international export regulations regarding security, privacy, and encryption-capable consumer software.

## Scope
Documents the export control classification (ECCN) and legal restrictions regarding the distribution of the cryptographic features of NEXUS MESH.

## ECCN Classification
NEXUS MESH contains point-to-point and end-to-end cryptographic software utilizing asymmetric key agreements (ECDH) and symmetric bulk encryption (AES-GCM-256).
- **US ECCN**: 5D992.c (Mass Market Software containing encryption)
- **HS Code**: 8523.49
- **Legal Notice**: This software is eligible for export under License Exception ENC (Section 740.17) of the US Export Administration Regulations.
