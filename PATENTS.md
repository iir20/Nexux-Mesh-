# Patent License Grant & Retaliation Agreement

## Purpose
Establishes the patent conditions, reciprocal licensing agreements, and termination provisions protecting users and contributors of the NEXUS MESH protocol.

## Scope
Defines the terms of the reciprocal patent licensing under Section 3 of the Apache License 2.0 and the legal protections regarding patent litigation.

## Reciprocal Grant
Each contributor to NEXUS MESH grants a perpetual, worldwide, non-exclusive, royalty-free, irrevocable patent license to users for the contributions they submit.

## Defensive Termination
If You institute patent litigation against any entity claiming that the NEXUS MESH Work constitutes direct or contributory patent infringement, then any patent licenses granted to You under this License for that Work shall terminate as of the date such litigation is filed.
